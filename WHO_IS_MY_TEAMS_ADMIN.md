# How to Find Your Teams Admin

## Method 1: Check Microsoft 365 Admin Portal

If you have access to https://admin.microsoft.com:
- Look for "Teams Admin" role
- Check who has this role in your organization

## Method 2: Ask IT Help Desk

Email or call your IT Help Desk:
- "Who is the Microsoft Teams administrator for our organization?"
- "I need to publish a custom Teams app, who can help?"

## Method 3: Check Common Admin Emails

Try these common patterns at your organization:
- teamsadmin@merck.com
- ithelpdesk@merck.com
- servicedesk@merck.com
- o365admin@merck.com

## Method 4: Ask Your Manager

Your manager likely knows who handles Teams app approvals
